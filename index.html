Project Objectives

1. Sales Analysis

  • What is the total sales amount by region, state, and city?
  • Which products, categories, and sub-categories are the top performers in terms of sales?
  • What are the seasonal sales trends, and when do sales peak or drop throughout the year?
  • How are orders distributed across different sales categories?
  • How much revenue is generated by each product category and sub-category?

2. Customer Segmentation

  • Who are our customers when segmented by type (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)?
  • Which customers contribute the most to overall sales?
  • How are the segments distributed across different seasons?
  • What is the order frequency for each customer segment?
  • Which customers contribute the most to the overall number of orders?

3. Product Performance

  • How many products are sold over time (year, month, day)?
  • Which product categories generate the most sales over time?
  • What is the total number of products sold?
  • What is the average order frequency across all products?

Python Data Transformation

order_counts = df.groupby('Customer ID')['Order ID'].nunique().reset_index()
order_counts.columns = ['Customer ID', 'Order Frequency']
df = df.merge(order_counts, on='Customer ID', how='left')

q_low = df['Sales'].quantile(0.33)
q_high = df['Sales'].quantile(0.66)

def categorize_sales(sale):
    if sale <= q_low:
        return 'Low'
    elif sale <= q_high:
        return 'Medium'
    else:
        return 'High'

df['Sales Category'] = df['Sales'].apply(categorize_sales)

SQL Query: Sales by Region


SELECT city, state, Region, sum(sales) AS Total_Sales
FROM [Final-project by Excel]

Key Findings

Sales by Category

Office Supplies leads in sales volume, followed by Furniture and Technology.

Regional Performance

The West region contributes the highest revenue, followed by East, Central, and South.

Customer Segments

Consumer segment has the highest order volume, though Corporate generates more revenue per order.

Seasonal Trends

Q4 shows consistently higher sales across all product categories, likely due to holiday shopping.

Recommendations

1. Focus on Office Supplies

Invest more in marketing and expanding the Office Supplies category, which shows the highest sales volume.

2. Prioritize West Region

Allocate more resources to the West region, which shows the highest revenue generation.

3. Seasonal Strategy

Develop specific seasonal promotional strategies for Q4, when sales peak across all categories.

4. Corporate Customer Focus

Create targeted loyalty programs for corporate customers who generate higher revenue per order.

5. Product Line Expansion

Consider expanding the top-performing sub-categories to capture more market share.

6. Optimize Shipping

Review shipping processes to reduce the delivery time gap and improve customer satisfaction.
