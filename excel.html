Excel Data Analysis

This section presents the data from our Excel analysis.

Dataset Overview

The final project Excel dataset contains comprehensive sales data with the following key columns:

  • Order information (ID, Date, Ship Date)
  • Customer details (ID, Name, Segment)
  • Geographic information (Country, City, State, Region)
  • Product details (ID, Category, Sub-Category, Name)
  • Sales figures
  • Additional derived fields from our analysis (Season, Order Day Name, etc.)

Key Findings from Excel Analysis

Sales by Category

Office Supplies leads in sales volume, followed by Furniture and Technology.

Regional Performance

The West region contributes the highest revenue, followed by East, Central, and South.

Customer Segments

Consumer segment has the highest order volume, though Corporate generates more revenue per order on average.

Seasonal Trends

Q4 shows consistently higher sales across all product categories, likely due to holiday shopping.
